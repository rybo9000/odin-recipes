Chicken

Description

This declious recipe will take you right back to your childhood at Grandma's house! It tastes savory and warm. Perfect for those cold winter nights.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • Lemons
  • Paprika
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Put the chicken in a deep pan
  2. Add chicken broth and lemons
  3. Let sit for 10 hours
  4. Add Paprika and Garlic
  5. Bake for 10 hours
