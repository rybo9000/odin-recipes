Nachos

Description

Live the bachelor lifestyle dream with this simple but tasty dish. Crack a beer and turn up the game because it's about to get cheesy in here!

Ingredients

  • Chips/li>
  • Cheese
  • Jalapenos
  • Refried Beans

Steps

  1. Put the chips onto your plate
  2. Add cheese on top of the chips
  3. Add Jalapenos
  4. Add refriend beans
  5. Put in the microwave on high for 90 seconds
