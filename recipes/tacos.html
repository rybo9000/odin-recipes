Tacos

Description

Get ready to experience life south of the border with this declious dish! These tacos are spicy and satisfying. Tuesday nights just got better!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until finished
  2. Put beef into tortilla
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add tomatoes
  5. Add lettuce
